Partners

Join us in building a healthier world!

Lose It! offers three partnership programs geared towards technology partners, health professionals, and corporate/strategic partners. Each of these programs offers unique opportunities for individuals and companies to benefit from using, promoting, and selling Lose It! products and features.

If you're interested in learning more about how you or your company can get involved, we'd love to hear from you.

Technology Partners

Do you have an innovative app, program or device that can assist our users in reaching their weight loss goals? Lose It! now offers an API that enables developers to transform their health and fitness apps, devices, and programs through the exchanging of Lose It! data to help our mutual users reach their goals.

Health Professional

Dietitians, Trainers, Weight Loss Coaches, Doctors, and other health professionals can connect to clients on Lose It! through Ascend for Lose It!. Add it to your portfolio of value-added services, and start coaching clients through the leading weight loss app.

Corporate/Strategic Partnerships

Lose It! is actively forming partnerships with leading brands and service providers that can assist our community in reaching their weight loss and health goals.
